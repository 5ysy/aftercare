# Severity Model

## Critical
Likely production failure, data loss, security exposure, or merge should stop.

## High
Strong architecture drift, boundary damage, or pipeline fragility that is likely to increase future change cost quickly.

## Medium
Meaningful maintainability issue that deserves a plan soon but might not block merge.

## Low
Useful cleanup, consistency, or naming improvement.

## Rule
Severity is not about how ugly something looks.
Severity is about cost, risk, and likely downstream damage.
